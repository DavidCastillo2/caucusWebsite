Welcome to NCF's caucus website!

Please select your role by clicking on the bubble with your role's name:

Administrator
An Administrator is someone who has control over the caucus such deleting and adding candidates and setting how many realignments there are.
Data Analystist
A Data Analystist is someone who can see how many votes was recieved for each candidate and other pieces of data related to this caucus.
Volunteer
A Volunteer counts the number of people voting for a candidate during a caucus.
